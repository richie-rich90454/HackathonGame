Introduction

Welcome to "Hackathon Game," a browser-based adventure game where you pilot an orb for collecting elemental orbs to trigger powerful reactions and earn points. Your objective is to reach a score of 10,000 points in the shortest amount of time by skillfully navigating and combining elements to speed up this process.

Controls

    `
  • Vertical Movement:
    • Keyboard: Press "W" to move up, "S" to move down.
    • Touch: Swipe up or tap the ▲ button to move up, swipe down or tap the ▼ button to move down.
  • Speed Adjustment:
    • Keyboard: Press "-" or "A" to decrease maximum speed, "="", "+", or "D" to increase maximum speed.
    • Touch: Tap the "-" button to slow down, "+"" button to speed up.

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Collecting Orbs:
    • Maneuver your orb to collect elemental orbs that appear across the terrain.
    • Elements include: Feuer (Fire), Wasser (Water), Elektro (Electro), Rasen (Grass), Kryo (Ice), Gestein (Rock), and Luft (Air).
  • Elemental Reactions:
    • Collecting specific combinations of orbs triggers reactions that boost your score.
    • Reactions fall into categories: amplifying (multiplies points), transformative (adds flat bonuses), catalyze (enhances points), or status (applies effects).
  • Timer System:
    • Collecting an orb may apply a timer to your orb, lasting for 4 seconds.
    • The next orb you collect can react with your last-collected element to trigger a reaction.
    • If no reaction occurs within the time limit, the timer ends and you could not perform the reaction.

Elemental Reactions List

Here are all possible reactions and their effects:

  • Vaporize (Feuer+Wasser or Wasser+Feuer):
    • Amplifying: Multiplies base points by 2 (Feuer then Wasser) or 1.5 (Wasser then Feuer).
  • Melt (Feuer+Kryo or Kryo+Feuer):
    • Amplifying: Multiplies base points by 2 (Feuer then Kryo) or 1.5 (Kryo then Feuer).
  • Overloaded (Elektro+Feuer or Feuer+Elektro):
    • Transformative: Adds 175 points.
  • Superconduct (Elektro+Kryo or Kryo+Elektro):
    • Transformative: Adds 60 points.
  • Elektro-Charged (Elektro+Wasser or Wasser+Elektro):
    • Transformative: Adds 90 points.
  • Swirl (Luft+Feuer/Wasser/Elektro/Kryo):
    • Transformative: Adds 60 points.
  • Crystallize (Gestein+Feuer/Wasser/Elektro/Kryo):
    • Transformative: Adds 40 points.
  • Burning (Feuer+Rasen or Rasen+Feuer):
    • Transformative: Adds 60 points.
  • Frozen (Wasser+Kryo or Kryo+Wasser):
    • Status: Adds 40 points.
  • Bloom (Wasser+Rasen or Rasen+Wasser):
    • Transformative: Adds 70 points.
  • Quicken (Rasen+Elektro or Elektro+Rasen):
    • Catalyze: Adds 60 points.
  • Aggravate (Elektro+Quicken):
    • Catalyze: Adds 200 points.
  • Spread (Rasen+Quicken):
    • Catalyze: Adds 200 points.
  • BurGesteinn (Feuer+Bloom):
    • Transformative: Adds 120 points.
  • Hyperbloom (Elektro+Bloom):
    • Transformative: Adds 120 points.

Scoring

  • Base Points: Each orb awards points based on its size (radius x 10).
  • Reaction Bonuses: Triggering reactions adds bonus points as listed above.
  • Total Score: Sum of base points and reaction bonuses; game ends at 10,000 points.

Winning the Game

Achieve a score of 10,000 points to win. Upon completion, you'll see your total score, time taken, and a list of reactions performed.

Tips and Strategies

  • Memorize reaction combinations to maximize your score.
  • Time your orb collection to trigger reactions before the timer ends expire.
