Introduction

Welcome to "Hackathon Game," a browser-based adventure game where you pilot an orb for collecting elemental orbs to trigger powerful reactions and earn points. Your objective is to reach a score of 10,000 points in the shortest amount of time by skillfully navigating and combining elements to speed up this process.

Controls

    `
  • Vertical Movement:
    • Keyboard: Press "W" to move up, "S" to move down.
    • Touch: Swipe up or tap the ▲ button to move up, swipe down or tap the ▼ button to move down.
  • Speed Adjustment:
    • Keyboard: Press "-" or "A" to decrease maximum speed, "="", "+", or "D" to increase maximum speed.
    • Touch: Tap the "-" button to slow down, "+"" button to speed up.

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Collecting Orbs:
    • Guide your core orb to absorb elemental fragments scattered across the terrain.
    • Available elements include: Pyra (Fire), Aqua (Water), Voltis (Electric), Flora (Nature), Glacia (Ice), Terra (Stone), and Aero (Wind).
  • Elemental Reactions:
    • Absorbing certain combinations of elements triggers special reactions that increase your score.
    • Reactions fall into categories:
      • Amplifying: Multiplies your base points based on element order.
      • Transformative: Grants a flat point bonus for combining unstable elements.
      • Catalytic: Enhances future interactions with bonus effects.
      • Status: Applies short-lived conditions and minor score boosts.
  • Energy Timer System:
    • Each absorbed element activates an energy window lasting 4 seconds.
    • If another element is collected within this window, it may trigger a reaction based on the combination.
    • If the timer expires without a match, no reaction occurs and the energy resets.

Elemental Reactions List

Here are all possible reactions and their effects:

  • Steamburst (Pyra+Aqua or Aqua+Pyra):
    • Amplifying: Multiplies base points by 2 (Pyra first) or 1.5 (Aqua first).
  • Frostburn (Pyra+Glacia or Glacia+Pyra):
    • Amplifying: Multiplies base points by 2 (Pyra first) or 1.5 (Glacia first).
  • Sparkflare (Voltis+Pyra or Pyra+Voltis):
    • Explosive: Triggers a blast that adds 175 points.
  • Chillshock (Voltis+Glacia or Glacia+Voltis):
    • Frictional: Reduces stability and adds 60 points.
  • Voltflow (Voltis+Aqua or Aqua+Voltis):
    • Conductive: Generates pulse damage, adding 90 points.
  • Galeweave (Aero+Pyra/Aqua/Voltis/Glacia):
    • Dispersive: Scatters energy and adds 60 points.
  • Stoneguard (Terra+Pyra/Aqua/Voltis/Glacia):
    • Shielding: Forms a protective shard and adds 40 points.
  • Wildfire (Pyra+Flora or Flora+Pyra):
    • Burning: Sustains damage and adds 60 points.
  • Icebind (Aqua+Glacia or Glacia+Aqua):
    • Status: Freezes target momentarily, adding 40 points.
  • Lifeburst (Aqua+Flora or Flora+Aqua):
    • Reactive Growth: Creates an energy pod that adds 70 points.
  • Growthspark (Flora+Voltis or Voltis+Flora):
    • Catalytic: Accelerates biological charge and adds 60 points.
  • Shockbloom (Voltis+Growthspark):
    • Chain Effect: Enhances with electric surge, adding 200 points.
  • Wildgrowth (Flora+Growthspark):
    • Chain Effect: Strengthens with rapid bloom, adding 200 points.
  • Flamebloom (Pyra+Lifeburst):
    • Detonative: Causes pod to combust, adding 120 points.
  • Surgebloom (Voltis+Lifeburst):
    • Electric Trigger: Zaps the pod into action, adding 120 points.

Scoring

  • Base Points: Each orb awards points based on its size (radius x 10).
  • Reaction Bonuses: Triggering reactions adds bonus points as listed above.
  • Total Score: Sum of base points and reaction bonuses; game ends at 10,000 points.

Winning the Game

Achieve a score of 10,000 points to win. Upon completion, you'll see your total score, time taken, and a list of reactions performed.

Tips and Strategies

  • Memorize reaction combinations to maximize your score.
  • Time your orb collection to trigger reactions before the timer ends expire.
